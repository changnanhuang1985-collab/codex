# AI Agent Weekly Review Template

## Purpose

This template is used to run a weekly review of the AI team as an operating system, not just a set of documents.

It helps observe:

- what moved
- what stalled
- what was learned
- what should be improved next week

## Review Structure

### 1. This Week's Focus

- What the team tried to achieve

### 2. Completed Outputs

- Key deliverables finished

### 3. In-Progress Items

- Work still moving
- Current blockers

### 4. Missed / Delayed Items

- What slipped
- Why it slipped

### 5. Cross-Role Collaboration Review

- Which roles worked well together
- Where coordination was weak

### 6. Proactivity Review

- Which agents acted proactively
- What triggered that action
- Was the proactivity useful or noisy

### 7. Learning Review

- What new knowledge was gained
- What should be turned into a template, checklist, or standard

### 8. Quality Review

- Was the output credible, concise, and useful
- What rework happened and why

### 9. Next Week Priorities

- Top 3 priorities

### 10. Owner Summary

- `大黄` summary
- `田内` follow-up actions

## Suggested Cadence

- Weekly
- Use for important weeks, customer preparation weeks, or project transitions
